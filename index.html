How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Word Ladder account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  1. Open the Word Ladder app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Profile icon in the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Immediate & Permanent  Deletion is processed immediately and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted with no retention period:

  • Your Firebase authentication account and user ID
  • All game progress (puzzles, scores, streaks) for all difficulty levels
  • Your leaderboard name and all leaderboard entries
  • Your push notification token and notification preferences
  • Your premium purchase status record
  • All other profile data stored on our servers

What Is Not Retained

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. No backups of individual user records are stored. Third-party services (Firebase, RevenueCat, Google AdMob) may retain anonymized or aggregated data in accordance with their own privacy policies; we have no control over this data.

Contact

If you cannot access the app and need your account deleted, or if you have any questions, please email us at vladfantasyfootball@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will process it manually within 7 days.
